Classification Scoring System (0-100 points)

Each article is scored by scanning its title and summary for security-related keywords and geopolitical entities. The final score determines its threat level.

Security Keywords

High-priority (+15 pts each): military, conflict, war, attack, missile, nuclear, sanctions, invasion, coup, terrorism, South China Sea, sovereignty, territorial, maritime dispute, cyber attack, espionage, ASEAN, Indo-Pacific, Quad, AUKUS (+ equivalents in French, Russian, Chinese, Korean, Japanese)

Medium-priority (+8 pts each): diplomacy, treaty, alliance, defense, strategic, geopolitics, trade war, tariff, embargo, human rights, refugee, Belt and Road, submarine, aircraft carrier, ambassador, summit

Low-priority (+3 pts each): economy, trade, investment, cooperation, technology, climate, education, tourism

Geopolitical Entities

Critical (+20 pts): Vietnam, China, USA, South China Sea, Taiwan, North Korea

High (+10 pts): ASEAN, Japan, South Korea, Australia, India, Russia, Philippines, Indonesia, Thailand, Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar, Malaysia, Singapore

Medium (+5 pts): EU, NATO, United Nations, G7, G20, France, Germany, UK

Threat Levels

CRITICAL Score >= 60 - Directly threatens Vietnam security or involves critical entities with high-severity keywords

HIGH Score 35-59 - Significant security implications for the region

MEDIUM Score 15-34 - Moderate relevance to foreign security

LOW Score < 15 - General international news with limited security impact

Vietnam Relevance

DIRECT Article explicitly mentions Vietnam, Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, South China Sea/Bien Dong, Spratly/Paracel islands

INDIRECT Article mentions regional entities (ASEAN, Mekong, Southeast Asia, neighboring countries) AND contains impact-related keywords

Vietnam Impact Categories

Articles related to Vietnam are further categorized into 6 impact areas: Maritime & Territorial, Military & Security, Trade & Economy, Diplomacy & Alliances, Regional Stability, Technology & Cyber
